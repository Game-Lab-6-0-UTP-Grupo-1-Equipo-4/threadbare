# Create your game pitch deck

### Why this task matters

A pitch is a concise and compelling presentation that outlines the concept and key features of a video game, and it's a common practice in the video game industry. It serves as a persuasive document meant to generate interest, secure funding, and ultimately get a project greenlit for production... _**or get your StoryQuest added to Threadbare!**_
So prepare your presentation, and get ready to wow your audience!

### Steps

1. **Get familiar with the content of a pitch**:
Even though there’s no standard for what a video game pitch must have, commonly there are some key points that need to be communicated in it:

- > Story, genre, and core mechanics of the game
- > Unique selling points
- > Target demographic
- > Concept art or images from the prototype

2. **See the examples** below in order to get a sense of how pitches look and work on the video game industry:

- [Gaming Pitch Deck Examples](https://bestpitchdeck.com/gaming/)

- [How To Pitch Games and Ideas](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ofZQrl01yaU)

- [Jason Della Rocca’s Guide To Pitching Video Games](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r8HSxYAE3Cs&t=124s)

3. **Take the template provided** below (see _Materials_), and make a **copy** for your project. Take into account:
a. Only one team member should generate the copy, and then grant edit access to the rest of the team, so you can all collaborate. 
b. Don't be afraid to go off script! Make the pitch template your own! Just keep in mind the key points mentioned above.
